Why we're here

No single front door

  • Contracts arrived via email, Slack DMs, direct messages to Hamraj
  • No consistent way to submit — every AE did it differently
  • Easy for requests to get lost or missed entirely

Zero visibility

  • No central tracker — Legal worked from their inbox
  • AEs had no idea where their contract was in the process
  • Chasing Hamraj directly for updates was the only option

No reporting

  • No data on volume, turnaround times, or workload
  • Impossible to spot bottlenecks or plan resourcing
  • No record of what came in, when, or how long it took
The fix

One email address, automatic tracking in Notion, Slack notifications for everyone involved, and a weekly report so Legal always has the full picture.
